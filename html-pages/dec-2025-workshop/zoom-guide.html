Basic Zoom Controls

Once you're in the meeting, here are the important buttons you'll use.

üé§

Mute / Unmute (Your Microphone)

In the bottom left corner of your screen, you'll see a microphone icon.

‚Ä¢ Microphone with a red line through it = You are MUTED (others can't hear you)

‚Ä¢ Microphone without a line = You are UNMUTED (others can hear you)

Click the microphone icon to switch between muted and unmuted.

üîá Important

During the workshop, please stay muted unless you're asked to speak. This prevents background noise from disrupting the presentation.

üìπ

Video On / Off (Your Camera)

Next to the microphone, you'll see a camera icon.

‚Ä¢ Camera with a red line through it = Your video is OFF (others can't see you)

‚Ä¢ Camera without a line = Your video is ON (others can see you)

Click the camera icon to turn your video on or off.

üí° Tip

We encourage you to turn your video ON so we can see your smiling face! But it's okay to leave it off if you prefer.

üí¨

Chat (Send Messages)

Look at the bottom of your screen for a button that says "Chat" (it may look like a speech bubble).

Step 1: Click "Chat" to open the chat panel on the right side of your screen.

Step 2: Type your message in the box at the bottom.

Step 3: Press Enter on your keyboard (or tap Send) to send it.

The chat is a great way to ask questions or share your thoughts without interrupting!

Virtual Background

A virtual background replaces whatever is behind you with an image. Great for privacy or fun!

A

Set Up BEFORE Joining a Meeting

Step 1: Open the Zoom app on your computer.

Step 2: Click the gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è (Settings) in the top right corner.

Step 3: Click "Background & Effects" in the left menu.

Step 4: You'll see some built-in backgrounds. Click one to try it!

Step 5: To add your own image, click the "+" button and select an image from your computer.

B

Change Background DURING a Meeting

Step 1: Click the small arrow ^ next to the camera/video icon at the bottom.

Step 2: Click "Choose Virtual Background" or "Background & Effects".

Step 3: Select the background you want.

Step 4: Close the settings window. Your new background is now active!

Troubleshooting

Having issues? Here are solutions to common problems.

1. Check your computer/phone volume. Make sure it's not muted or turned all the way down.

2. Check Zoom's audio settings. Click the small arrow next to the microphone icon, then "Audio Settings". Make sure the correct speaker is selected.

3. Try clicking "Test Speaker" in the audio settings to hear if sound is working.

4. Close and reopen Zoom. Sometimes this fixes audio issues.

5. Restart your computer/device if nothing else works.

3. Test your microphone. In audio settings, speak and watch the bar next to "Input Level". If it moves, your mic is working.

4. Check if other apps can use your microphone. Close other apps that might be using it.

2. Make sure video is turned on. Check for the red line through the camera icon.

3. Check Zoom's video settings. Click the arrow next to the camera icon, then "Video Settings". Make sure the correct camera is selected.

4. Close other apps that might be using your camera (like FaceTime or another video app).

5. Restart Zoom and try again.
