Six Categories of Transformation

Track your progress across these essential areas of your life

Category 1

Who I Am

Define your essence identity. Who are you when operating from certainty and confidence? Your authentic self beyond roles and labels.

Category 2

Friday Night Essence (FNE)

Capture what excites you most. What do you want to do when obligations are complete? Your deepest desires and passions.

Category 3

My Dreams

List your impossible dreams. What would you create if you operated from certainty without fear? Your ultimate vision.

Category 4

Current Focuses

Track your active projects and goals. What are you working on now to move toward your dreams? Actionable focus areas.

Category 5

Celebrations (Value Reflection)

Acknowledge value created. What genuine value did you create for others? Measure wealth through contribution.

Category 6

Health & Longevity

Track physical and mental vitality. What actions support your desire to live indefinitely? Health as prerequisite for creation.

How to Use Your Golden Life Sheet

1

Daily Morning Review

Start each day by reviewing your six categories. Reconnect with your essence, dreams, and current focuses. Set intention for value creation.

2

Evening Reflection

End each day by updating your celebrations. What value did you create? What progress did you make? Acknowledge wins, however small.

3

Weekly Deep Dive

Once per week, review and update all six categories. Are your focuses aligned with your dreams? Is your FNE clear? Adjust as needed.

4

Monthly Evolution

At month end, notice transformation patterns. How have your dreams evolved? What new capabilities emerged? Celebrate growth systematically.
