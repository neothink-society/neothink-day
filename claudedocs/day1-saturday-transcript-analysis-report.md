## PART 3: PEGGY SUE'S FRIDAY NIGHT ESSENCE GUIDED MEDITATION

### Time: ~3:00pm PT - 4:00pm PT

**Facilitator:** Peggy Sue (Success Guide, meditation and hypnotherapist)
**Purpose:** Guide participants to discover the common thread of joy running through their entire life
**Methodology:** Guided visualization journey through specific life stages

### MEDITATION STRUCTURE

**Introduction:**
- Find comfortable seated position
- Awakening breath work (4 counts in, hold 4, out 4)
- Full body relaxation scan (head to toes)
- State: "Alert and relaxed. Present and open. Ready to discover."

### AGE 6-8 (CHILDHOOD)

**Prompts Given:**
- Drift back to ages 6, 7, or 8 years old
- See your childhood home (front door, windows, colors, yard, steps)
- Step inside - where did you spend most time? Favorite room? Special corner?
- **Free time play:** "What do you do naturally? What do you navigate towards?"
- **Time-loss activities:** "What activities make you lose track of time?"
- Options explored: Building something? Drawing? Making up stories? Outside exploring? Playing with toys (which ones, how)?
- **Body feeling:** "Notice how it feels in your body - energized? Peaceful? Completely absorbed?"

**Adult Observations:**
- "What did adults say you were good at?"
- "What did they notice about you?"
- Fill in the blanks:
  - "She's so good at _____"
  - "He can spend hours doing _____"
  - "You should see the way they can _____"
  - "They have such a gift for _____"
- Don't dismiss these memories - children are naturally good at things that matter, connected to who they really are

**School Day:**
- What does backpack look like? What color? What's inside?
- Journey to school (walk, bus, ride?)
- In classroom: Where do you sit? What's on walls? Teacher's name? What they looked like?
- **Subject that lit you up:** Which class made time fly? (Recess, storytime, science experiments, building, writing?)
- Friends: Who are they? What did you love doing together?

### AGE 11-13 (PRE-TEEN/EARLY TEEN)

**Home Environment:**
- See where you lived (same home or moved?)
- Your room - what's in it? What's on walls?

**Free Time:**
- Weekend free time: "What do you choose to do?"
- "When no one is directing your activities, where does attention naturally go?"
- **Hobbies captured:** "What could you do for hours without getting bored?"
- "What made you forget to come inside for dinner?"

**School Experience:**
- Middle school changes
- Backpack appearance, how you get to school
- **Classes you looked forward to**
- **When time seemed to disappear:** Solving problems? Creating? Performing? Discussing ideas? Working with hands?

**Social:**
- Friends now - what do you love doing together?
- Sleepovers/after school - what activities do you gravitate towards?

**Adult Recognition:**
- What did parents, teachers, coaches, mentors notice about your natural abilities?
- "What came easily to you that others seemed to struggle with?"
- Fill-in phrases:
  - "They're really good at _____"
  - "You should see what they can do _____"
  - "They've always been talented at _____"
  - "I've never seen anyone at their age who can _____"

### AGE 16-17 (MID-TEEN)

**The Friday Night Question:**

**Key Insight:**
