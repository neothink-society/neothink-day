## Automation Workflow Logic

### Timeline-Based Triggers

The automation should work as follows:

1. **Upon Registration** ‚Üí Send Registration Confirmation immediately
2. **Upon VIP Purchase** ‚Üí Send VIP Confirmation immediately
3. **9 Days Before Event** ‚Üí Send 9-Day Prep Email
4. **4 Days Before Event** ‚Üí Send 4-Day Calendar Email
5. **3 Days Before Event** ‚Üí Send 3-Day VIP Email
6. **2 Days Before Event** ‚Üí Send 2-Day Checklist Email
7. **1 Day Before Event (evening)** ‚Üí Send 1-Day Logistics Email
8. **Day Of Event @ 7am PT** ‚Üí Send Day-Of Zoom Link Email

### Late Registrations

**If someone registers LESS than 9 days before the event:**
- Send Registration Confirmation immediately
- Catch them up to the appropriate email in the sequence based on current date
- Example: Register 5 days before ‚Üí Get Registration Confirmation + 4-Day Email immediately

### VIP Deadline

**VIP Upgrade Closes**: 8:00 AM PT on Saturday, November 22, 2025
- All emails before 3-day email promote VIP upgrade
- 3-day email is the MAIN VIP conversion push
- 2-day and 1-day emails mention VIP upgrade closing soon
- Day-of email: VIP upgrade no longer available
