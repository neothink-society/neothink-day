Setup Instructions

Desktop (Mac/PC)

  1. Download the background image (click button above)
  2. Open Zoom and click the Settings gear icon
  3. Go to Background & Effects
  4. Click + and select Add Image
  5. Choose your downloaded VIP background
  6. Click to activate — you're all set!

Mobile (iPhone/Android)

  1. Download the image to your phone
  2. Open Zoom and join the workshop
  3. Tap More (...) in the bottom right
  4. Select Virtual Background
  5. Tap + to add a new background
  6. Choose your VIP background from photos — done!
