### Timezone Settings
- **CRITICAL**: Set all automations to **Pacific Time (PT)**
- December is PST (Pacific Standard Time) - no DST

### Automation Logic
1. Registration triggers immediate confirmation email
2. VIP purchase triggers immediate VIP confirmation
3. All countdown emails triggered by "days before event" calculation
4. Use "Wait until specific time" workflow step for each send time

### Late Registration Handling

**NOVEMBER (Bridge + Countdown Structure):**
- **Register Thu Nov 13 - Sun Nov 16**: Get Bridge emails ‚Üí Sync to countdown Mon Nov 17
- **Register Mon Nov 17 or later**: Skip bridge emails ‚Üí Catch up to current countdown day

Examples:
- Register Thu Nov 13 ‚Üí Welcome immediately ‚Üí Methodology +24h ‚Üí Day 5 on Mon Nov 17
- Register Sat Nov 15 ‚Üí Welcome immediately ‚Üí Methodology +24h ‚Üí Day 5 on Mon Nov 17
- Register Sun Nov 16 ‚Üí Welcome immediately ‚Üí Skip methodology ‚Üí Day 5 on Mon Nov 17
- Register Mon Nov 17 ‚Üí Skip bridge ‚Üí Day 5 immediately
- Register Tue Nov 18 ‚Üí Skip bridge ‚Üí Day 4 immediately
- Register Wed Nov 19 ‚Üí Skip bridge ‚Üí Day 3 immediately

- Register 18 days before ‚Üí Send Day 20 email immediately
- Register 12 days before ‚Üí Send Day 14 email immediately
