Session Overview

This was the final comprehensive training session before Friday's rehearsal. Marco Hamilton led an in-depth review of the complete call flow, common scenarios, and how to handle various member situations. The emphasis was on mastering the success guide mindset and being prepared for anything that might come up during the live event.

Complete Call Flow (Detailed)

Remember: Wallace gets them in buying mode. They already know about the offer when they come to you.

  1. Rapport Building (1-2 minutes): "Hi [name]! Great to meet you. I'm [Your Name], I'm a success guide with the society. I've been a member for [X years]."
    • "Where are you joining us from?"
    • IMPORTANT: "How long have you been a member?" (Helps you understand context - new member vs. long-time member)
  2. Set the Frame: "I'm a society success guide and mentor, here to make sure everything is super simple for you, answer any questions, and help you get set up and ascend if you're ready."
  3. Decision Check (UPDATED): "So, let me know where you're at. Have you already made the decision to join and just need help with the payment or link?"
    • ‚ö†Ô∏è Don't ask if they need "more clarification" - this can provoke concerns prematurely
    • ‚úÖ If they have questions, they'll volunteer them naturally
  4. Handle Their Response: See "Most Common Scenarios" below for specific approaches
  5. Process Payment (if ready): "Perfect! I can actually go ahead and help you process that on my end to make sure there's no problems and we get you all set up and ready to go smoothly."
    • "How would you like to pay? Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express?"
    • This question (learned from Grant Cardone) gives them options while assuming the sale
    • 9 times out of 10, they'll just tell you what card they're using

Most Common Scenarios

Scenario #1: Inquiring About Payment Plans (MOST COMMON)

Response: "We have three payment options available:"

  • Full payment: $1,997 (best value)
  • 2-payment: $1,000 today, $1,000 in 30 days
  • 3-payment: $667 today, $667 in 30 days, $667 in 60 days

"Which one works best for you?"

Scenario #2: Can't Afford Regular Payment Plans (SOMEWHAT COMMON)

When: They want to ascend but can't do $667 first payment

Response: "I see how serious and committed you are, and I know how this can transform your life. I don't want that first payment stopping you from missing the deal and your opportunity to ascend - because this offer does expire Wednesday. What I would like to do, and we don't normally do this, is set up some sort of custom plan. That way we can lock you in right now and make sure you're able to ascend."

Then ask: "What kind of payment structure feels realistic for you right now? That way we can get you signed up today."

  • ‚ö†Ô∏è KEY: They need to put something down NOW (ideally at least $200)
  • Exception: "I have no money now, but Tuesday I get a check and can pay it all" - this can work
  • Next step: "Let me get approval from the team..." ‚Üí Post in WhatsApp group chat
  • Marco, Josh, or team member will hop into breakout room to handle custom plan
Scenario #3: Quick Clarification Questions

Common questions:

  • "When is the event?" ‚Üí For Level 3 Stargazer events, dates TBD (will be coordinated with Level 3 members)
  • "How long is it?" ‚Üí 3-day event (Stargazer)
  • "What exactly do I get?" ‚Üí Wallace will cover this, but main value is access to 3-day Stargazer event + bonuses

Note: More detailed offer breakdown coming from Wallace by Friday

Scenario #4: "I Want to Buy But Not Now" / Hesitation

Approach: Go into discovery mode - get curious about them

  • "I totally understand. I'm just curious - what were you looking to get out of this event?"
  • "What is it that you're looking to get out of ascending to Level 3?"
  • Goal: Get them to sell themselves by articulating their own reasons

Then use the guarantee:

"I totally understand you're busy/not sure. And we do have very strong guarantees. In fact, one of our guarantees is: If you ascend now and attend the 3-day event, you can attend the first day. If you don't see at least 5 times the value of what you paid after the first day, you can get a full refund, no questions asked."

Extended guarantee: "And if for whatever reason you can't make that event, we'll have another one in the future, and the money-back guarantee is extended all the way until you attend your first day. So even if you don't make the next one, you still have the money-back guarantee until Day 2 of whichever event you attend."

If still not ready: That's okay - not everyone will buy. Post situation in WhatsApp group chat. Help them schedule a follow-up call with Josh.

Success Guides Mindset & Role

  • You Are NOT Salespeople: You are Success Guides - mentors helping members make the right decision for themselves

Payment Processing Mechanics

  • 2-Payment Plan: $1,000 today, $1,000 in 30 days (total: $2,000)
  • 3-Payment Plan: $667 today, $667 in 30 days, $667 in 60 days (total: $2,001)
  • Custom Plans: Available for committed members - minimum $200 down payment, custom schedule
  • Payment Collection: MUST be collected DURING the call - never send payment links for later

Call Flow for "Ready to Buy" Scenario

This is the primary scenario you'll encounter (majority of calls):

  1. Rapport Building (1-2 minutes): "Hi [name]! Great to connect with you. How are you enjoying the event so far?"
  2. Frame the Call as Success Guide: "I'm here as your Success Guide to help you with whatever you need. What brought you into this breakout room?"
  3. Decision Check: "Have you already decided you want to ascend to Level 3, or do you need more information about what's included?"
  4. Present Payment Options (if ready to buy): "Great! Let me walk you through your payment options..."
    • Full payment: $1,997 (best value)
    • 2-payment: $1,000 today, $1,000 in 30 days
    • 3-payment: $667 today, $667 in 30 days, $667 in 60 days
    • Custom plan: Available if needed (discuss with team first)
  5. Process Payment: "Perfect! I'm going to pull up the payment form and walk you through it step by step..."
    • Share payment form link
    • Walk through each field together
    • Collect card information
    • Process payment in real-time
  6. Congratulations & Excitement: "Congratulations on your ascension to Level 3! You've made an incredible decision. You're going to love the Stargazer experience..."
  7. Document in Group Chat: Share in WhatsApp group: "[Member Name] just ascended to Level 3! [Payment plan used]"

Handling Special Scenarios

  • Payment Declined: Try the card again, ask if they have another card, or discuss custom payment plan options
  • Needs Custom Plan: Minimum $200 down payment required - check WhatsApp group chat for approval from Marco/Josh/Will before processing
  • Technical Issues: Stay on the line with member, message group chat for immediate support
  • Wants to Think About It: Respect their decision, share resources, offer to reconnect later during event

Money-Back Guarantee

  • Risk-Free Promise: If members don't get 5X their investment value on Day 1 alone, they get a full refund
  • Confidence Tool: Use this to address hesitation - "You have nothing to lose and everything to gain"
  • No Pressure: This removes all risk from their decision

Communication & Support During Events

  • WhatsApp Group Chat: Real-time coordination with Marco, Josh, Will, and all Success Guides
  • Share Wins: Post every successful ascension to celebrate and motivate the team
  • Ask Questions: No question is too small - leadership is monitoring chat constantly
  • Escalate When Needed: If you're stuck, tag leadership immediately for help
