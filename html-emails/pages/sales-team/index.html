üíº Your Role as Sales Team

As volunteer sales team members, you'll receive a special bonus for representing The Neothink Society during these events. Here's what you'll be doing:

  • Camera on entire time - Be visible and present throughout the event
  • Keep chat active and positive - Respond to questions and maintain high energy
  • Support Level 3 ascension - Help members who want to ascend to the next level
  • Manage breakout rooms - Pull people into private conversations for assistance
  • Experience the event yourself - Fully participate while supporting others

üí° Two Main Sales Scenarios

Scenario 1: Ready to Ascend (Majority of cases)

  • Members have already decided they want to ascend to Level 3
  • They need help processing their payment
  • They want to discuss payment plan options
  • Quick assistance to complete their purchase

Scenario 2: Seeking Information

  • Members are highly interested but need more information
  • They have questions before making their decision
  • Requires more preparation and product knowledge
  • Opportunity to guide them through the value proposition
