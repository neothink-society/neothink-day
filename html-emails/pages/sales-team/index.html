Session Notes

You Are NOT Salespeople

  • Success Guides: Mentors helping members make the right decision for themselves
  • Servant Leadership: Support, not pressure. Guide with genuine care
  • Your Experience: Fully participate in the event while helping members who reach out
  • Event Structure: 2-day virtual experience, hundreds of attendees
  • Level 3 Stargazer: $1,997 full payment OR $2,000 with payment plans
  • 2-Payment: $1,000 today + $1,000 in 30 days
  • 3-Payment: $667 today + $667 in 30 days + $667 in 60 days
  • Custom Plans: Minimum $250 down, custom schedule (get approval first)
  • CRITICAL: Payment MUST be collected DURING the call - never send links for later

This is the primary scenario you'll encounter (majority of calls):

  1. Rapport (1-2 min): "Hi [name]! Great to connect. How are you enjoying the event?"
  2. Frame as Success Guide: "I'm here to help you with whatever you need. What brought you into this room?"
  3. Decision Check: "Have you already decided to ascend, or do you need more info about what's included?"
  4. Present Options: Walk through full payment ($1,997), 2-payment, 3-payment, or custom
  5. Process Payment: Share form, walk through each field together, collect card info, process live
  6. Congratulations: Celebrate their ascension - "You've made an incredible decision!"
  7. Document: Share in WhatsApp: "[Name] just ascended! [Payment plan used]"

About 50% of calls - members who aren't sure yet:

  • Listen First: Understand their specific concerns and questions
  • Value Proposition: Help them see what Level 3 unlocks for their specific goals
  • Handle Objections: Address concerns with empathy and facts
  • Use the Guarantee: "Nothing to lose" - 5X value on Day 1 or full refund
  • If Still Unsure: Help them schedule a follow-up with Josh
  • Risk-Free Promise: If they don't get 5X value on Day 1 alone, full refund
  • Confidence Tool: Use this to address hesitation - removes all risk
  • Extended Timeline: Guarantee extends until Day 2 of whichever event they attend
  • No Pressure: This makes it easy for them to say yes
  • Payment Declined: Try again, ask for another card, or discuss custom plan
  • Needs Custom Plan: Min $250 down - check WhatsApp for approval from Marco/Josh/Will first
  • Technical Issues: Stay on the line, message group chat for immediate support
  • Wants to Think: Respect their decision, share resources, offer to reconnect later

Quick Scripts

"Hi [name]! Great to meet you. I'm [Your Name], I'm a success guide with the society. I've been a member for [X years]. Where are you joining us from?"

Key question: "How long have you been a member?" - Helps understand context.

"So, let me know where you're at. Have you already made the decision to join and just need help with the payment or link?"

Important: Don't ask if they need "more clarification" - this can provoke concerns prematurely.

"We have three payment options: Full payment is $1,997 which is the best value. Or we can split it into two payments of $1,000, or three monthly payments of $667. Which one works best for you?"
"Perfect! I can actually go ahead and help you process that on my end to make sure there's no problems and we get you all set up smoothly. How would you like to pay? Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express?"

Tip: This question (from Grant Cardone) gives options while assuming the sale. 9/10 times they'll just tell you their card.

"We have a very strong guarantee: If you ascend now and attend the 3-day event, if you don't see at least 5 times the value after the first day, you can get a full refund, no questions asked."

Extended: Guarantee extends until Day 2 of whichever event they attend, even if they miss the first one.

"I see how serious and committed you are, and I know how this can transform your life. I don't want that first payment stopping you. What I can do is set up a custom plan - what payment structure feels realistic for you right now?"

Key: Minimum $250 down. Post in WhatsApp for approval. Marco/Josh will join breakout to help.

"I totally understand. I'm just curious - what were you looking to get out of ascending to Level 3? What transformation are you hoping for?"

Goal: Get them to sell themselves by articulating their own reasons. Then use the guarantee. If still not ready, help them schedule follow-up with Josh.
