Quick Scripts

"Hi [name]! Great to meet you. I'm [Your Name], I'm a success guide with the society. I've been a member for [X years]. Where are you joining us from?"

Key question: "How long have you been a member?" - Helps understand context.

"So, let me know where you're at. Have you already made the decision to join and just need help with the payment or link?"

Important: Don't ask if they need "more clarification" - this can provoke concerns prematurely.

"The Level 3 Stargazer Event is normally $5,000, but for Day of Enlightenment attendees, we have a special commitment offer: You pay $1,997 today to lock in your spot, and the remaining $3,003 is only due after you make $50,000 or more from the secrets and techniques taught at the event. It's 100% on the honor system - we trust you to pay when you hit that $50K mark. We're committed to making you money BEFORE the rest is due. If you're a VIP member, your $187 is credited, so your upfront is $1,810. We can also split the upfront amount into installments if needed. Which option works best for you?"

Key: Emphasize honor system trust and "making you money first" - this is powerful differentiation.

"Perfect! I can actually go ahead and help you process that on my end to make sure there's no problems and we get you all set up smoothly. How would you like to pay? Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express?"

Tip: This question (from Grant Cardone) gives options while assuming the sale. 9/10 times they'll just tell you their card.

"We have a very strong guarantee: If you ascend now and attend the 3-day event, if you don't see at least 5 times the value after the first day, you can get a full refund, no questions asked."

Extended: Guarantee extends until Day 2 of whichever event they attend, even if they miss the first one.

"I understand. Let me ask you this: Instead of asking 'Can I afford this?' which closes your mind, let's shift to the millionaire mindset - 'How CAN I afford this?' Let me show you a few ways: First, we can split the payment into smaller installments. Second, you can actually make Level 3 completely free by inviting just 4 people per month who get selected and join Level 2 - you earn $250 per person, which is $1,000 per month. Do that for two months and your entire upfront cost is covered. Third, remember the remaining $3,003 isn't due until AFTER you make $50,000 from what we teach you. And if none of those work, I can set up a custom plan starting with as little as $250 down. What payment structure feels realistic for you right now?"

Key: Minimum $250 down. Post in WhatsApp for approval. Marko/Josh/Joe will join breakout to help. Use all three strategies: payment plans, referral system, and honor system.

"I totally understand. I'm just curious - what were you looking to get out of ascending to Level 3? What transformation are you hoping for?"

Goal: Get them to sell themselves by articulating their own reasons. Then use the guarantee. If still not ready, help them schedule follow-up with Josh.
